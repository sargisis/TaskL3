News Article Title

An earthquake of magnitude 7.2 struck a coastal town early this morning, causing widespread damage and triggering fears of a tsunami. The quake, centered just off the coast, shook buildings and knocked out power across the region. Authorities are assessing the extent of the damage and urging residents to evacuate to higher ground as a precautionary measure.

Emergency services are scrambling to respond to reports of collapsed buildings and infrastructure damage. There are no immediate reports of casualties, but rescue teams are working tirelessly to search for survivors trapped in the rubble.

The earthquake comes as a stark reminder of the region's vulnerability to seismic activity. Residents are advised to stay alert and follow instructions from local authorities. Updates on the situation will be provided as they become available.
